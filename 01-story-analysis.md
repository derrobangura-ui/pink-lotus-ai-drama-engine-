# 01 - Story Analysis

## Purpose

Break a raw story idea into its emotional engine, core conflict, genre promise, audience hook, and serial potential.

## Inputs

- Story idea or rough plot
- Target audience
- Desired tone
- Platform or format

## Outputs

- Story DNA summary
- Main conflict
- Character pressure points
- Episode potential
- Risks and improvement notes

## Prompt

Analyze this story idea for serialized AI drama. Identify the hook, emotional engine, central conflict, strongest audience promise, and what must be improved before production.
