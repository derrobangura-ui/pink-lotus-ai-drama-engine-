# 03 - Series Bible

## Purpose

Define the larger series world, rules, season arc, recurring conflicts, and long-term audience promise.

## Inputs

- Story DNA
- Character bible
- Target episode count
- Genre and tone

## Outputs

- Series premise
- World rules
- Season arc
- Main relationship arcs
- Recurring tension
- Episode engine

## Prompt

Create a series bible that can guide multiple episodes of an AI drama. Focus on conflict, emotional continuity, audience retention, and production clarity.
