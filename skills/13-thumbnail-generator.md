# 13 - Thumbnail Generator

## Purpose

Create clickable thumbnail concepts that communicate emotion, conflict, and curiosity quickly.

## Inputs

- Episode summary
- Main conflict
- Character images or descriptions
- Platform

## Outputs

- Thumbnail concept
- Text options
- Facial expression direction
- Composition notes
- Color and contrast notes

## Prompt

Create thumbnail concepts for this episode. Focus on emotional clarity, readable conflict, curiosity, strong faces, and simple text.
