# Character Bible Template

## Character Name

Example: Amara Okeke

## Role in Story

Example: Main protagonist. She enters the wealthy Adeyemi household as a live-in assistant and becomes the person who threatens to expose their secrets.

## Age Range

Example: 24-26

## Personality

Example: Intelligent, sarcastic, guarded, hardworking, loyal, impatient with disrespect, emotionally softer than she wants people to know.

## Public Face

Example: Amara acts tough, confident, and unbothered. She uses jokes and sharp replies to hide when she feels humiliated.

## Private Wound

Example: She believes poor people are only loved when they are useful. Her father's death made her afraid of depending on anyone.

## Main Desire

Example: To save her mother, become financially independent, and earn dignity in a world that keeps looking down on her.

## Main Fear

Example: That she will sacrifice everything and still lose her mother. She also fears falling in love with someone who sees her as beneath him.

## Secret

Example: Amara has been quietly investigating the company that ruined her father, but she does not know the Adeyemi family is connected.

## Voice Style

Example: Quick, emotional, witty, direct. When angry, she becomes formal and cutting. When vulnerable, her words become simple and honest.

Sample line: "I may work in this house, but I did not sell my sense with my CV."

## Relationships

Example:

- Tobi Adeyemi: Attraction mixed with distrust.
- Chief Adeyemi: Employer and hidden enemy.
- Mrs. Adeyemi: Polite but manipulative mother figure.
- Kemi: Best friend and voice of street-smart advice.
- Mama Amara: Her emotional reason for fighting.

## Visual Notes

Example: Natural beauty, expressive eyes, neat but affordable clothes, simple braids, minimal jewelry. Her look should contrast with the mansion's luxury without making her appear weak.
