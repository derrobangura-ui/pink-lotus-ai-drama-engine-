# 12 - Video Prompt Generator

## Purpose

Create video generation prompts that capture motion, emotion, camera movement, and scene continuity.

## Inputs

- Scene direction
- Character descriptions
- Visual style
- Duration

## Outputs

- Video prompt
- Camera motion
- Character action
- Mood and lighting
- Continuity notes

## Prompt

Generate a video prompt for this animated drama scene. Include action, camera movement, expression, environment, pacing, and continuity details.
