# 04 - Episode Planner

## Purpose

Turn the series bible into focused episodes with clear beginnings, escalation, reversals, and endings.

## Inputs

- Series bible
- Episode number
- Current character arcs
- Required cliffhanger

## Outputs

- Episode premise
- Scene list
- Character goals
- Conflict beats
- Ending hook

## Prompt

Plan this episode with strong dramatic escalation. Make every scene change the emotional situation and end with a reason to watch the next episode.
