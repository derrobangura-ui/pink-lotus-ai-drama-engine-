# Story to Dialogue Workflow

1. Analyze the story with `skills/01-story-analysis.md`.
2. Build or update the character bible with `skills/02-character-bible.md`.
3. Plan the episode with `skills/04-episode-planner.md`.
4. Break the episode into scenes with `skills/05-scene-breakdown.md`.
5. Write the dialogue with `skills/06-dialogue-writer.md`.
6. Strengthen the ending with `skills/07-cliffhanger-engine.md`.
