# 14 - Social Content Generator

## Purpose

Turn episodes and story moments into short-form social posts, captions, teasers, and engagement prompts.

## Inputs

- Episode script or summary
- Target platform
- Audience
- Desired call to action

## Outputs

- Captions
- Short teasers
- Comment prompts
- Carousel ideas
- Hashtag suggestions

## Prompt

Create social content for this drama episode. Include captions, teaser posts, comment questions, and short hooks that make people want to watch.
