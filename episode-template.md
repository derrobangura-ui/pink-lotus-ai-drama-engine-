## Episode Number

## Episode Title

Example: The New Girl in the Mansion

## Logline

Example: Amara starts her first day working for the Adeyemi family, but a strange photograph in the mansion suggests her father's past may be connected to her new employers.

## Previous Episode Context

Example: This is the pilot episode, so there is no previous episode. Establish Amara's financial pressure, her mother's illness, and the intimidating world of the Adeyemi mansion.

## Main Conflict

Example: Amara needs to keep this job, but the family treats her like she should be silent, invisible, and grateful.

## Scene List

Example:

1. Amara argues with the hospital cashier about her mother's unpaid bill.
2. Kemi encourages Amara to accept the live-in job, even though it sounds suspicious.
3. Amara arrives at the Adeyemi mansion and is overwhelmed by the luxury.
4. Mrs. Adeyemi gives Amara strict rules and warns her not to enter the private study.
5. Tobi catches Amara standing up to a rude house manager and becomes interested.
6. Amara accidentally sees an old photograph of her father with Chief Adeyemi.

## Emotional Turns

Example:

- Amara begins desperate and embarrassed at the hospital.
- She becomes defensive when the mansion staff look down on her.
- She feels a small victory when she refuses to be insulted.
- She ends the episode shocked and suspicious after seeing her father's photo.

## Key Reveal

Example: Amara's late father knew Chief Adeyemi, even though her family never mentioned the connection.

## Cliffhanger

Example: Chief Adeyemi enters the room, sees Amara holding the photograph, and quietly says, "Where did you get that?"

## Production Notes

Example: Open with emotional realism, then shift into glossy mansion drama. Use close-ups for Amara's reactions, especially when she sees the photograph.
