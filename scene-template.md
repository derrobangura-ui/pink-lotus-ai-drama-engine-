# Scene Template

## Scene Number

Example: Scene 6

## Location

Example: Chief Adeyemi's private study, inside the Banana Island mansion.

## Characters

Example:

- Amara
- Chief Adeyemi
- Tobi, briefly at the doorway

## Scene Goal

Example: Amara discovers the first clue connecting her father to the Adeyemi family.

## Conflict

Example: Amara knows she is not allowed in the study, but curiosity and suspicion push her to search after she notices a familiar name on a document.

## Emotional Turn

Example: Amara moves from nervous curiosity to shock, then fear when Chief Adeyemi catches her.

## Action Beats

Example:

1. Amara enters the study to return a tray.
2. She notices a framed old photograph on the shelf.
3. She sees her father standing beside Chief Adeyemi in the photo.
4. Her hand shakes as she picks it up.
5. Tobi appears at the doorway and warns her to leave.
6. Chief Adeyemi enters before she can put the photo back.

## Dialogue Purpose

Example: The dialogue should reveal that Chief Adeyemi recognizes Amara's reaction, but he tries to control the situation without explaining anything.

Sample exchange:

Amara: "This man in the picture. Where do you know him from?"

Chief Adeyemi: "In this house, you ask only the questions your work requires."

## Visual Direction

Example: Use a quiet, tense mood. Focus on Amara's hand touching the photo, her eyes widening, and Chief Adeyemi's face hardening when he sees what she found.

## Ending Beat

Example: Chief Adeyemi takes the photograph from Amara and says, "Forget you saw this, if you want to keep your job."
