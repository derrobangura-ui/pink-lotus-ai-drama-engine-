# 15 - Quality Control

## Purpose

Review story, script, visuals, prompts, and social assets before production or publishing.

## Inputs

- Final script
- Prompts
- Episode plan
- Brand or channel standards

## Outputs

- Quality checklist
- Continuity issues
- Dialogue issues
- Visual issues
- Fix recommendations

## Prompt

Review this drama package for quality. Check story logic, character consistency, dialogue strength, cultural fit, visual clarity, engagement, and production readiness.
