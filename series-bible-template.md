# Series Bible Template

## Series Title

Example: The House on Banana Island

## Premise

Example: A struggling young woman takes a job inside a powerful Lagos family mansion and discovers that the people who seem to have everything are hiding crimes, betrayals, and emotional wounds that could destroy them.

## Genre and Tone

Example: Serialized drama with mystery, romance, class tension, family betrayal, and emotional cliffhangers. The tone is glossy, intense, and personal.

## World

Example: Modern Lagos, moving between Ajegunle, Lekki, Banana Island, hospitals, offices, parties, and private family spaces. The world should show the contrast between public success and private damage.

## Main Characters

Example:

- Amara Okeke: Protagonist, live-in assistant, secretly connected to the family's past.
- Tobi Adeyemi: Rebellious son, love interest, wants freedom from his father's control.
- Chief Bamidele Adeyemi: Powerful businessman hiding a major crime.
- Mrs. Ronke Adeyemi: Elegant matriarch who protects the family image at all costs.
- Kemi: Amara's best friend, funny and brutally honest.

## Season Arc

Example: Amara enters the mansion to survive, discovers clues about her father's death, falls for Tobi, and slowly realizes the Adeyemi family may be responsible for the tragedy that ruined her life.

## Episode Engine

Example: Every episode should combine one emotional conflict, one household secret, one class tension moment, and one cliffhanger reveal.

## Recurring Conflicts

Example:

- Amara's need for money vs. her need for truth
- Tobi's love for Amara vs. loyalty to his family
- Chief Adeyemi's public image vs. his buried crimes
- Mrs. Adeyemi's politeness vs. her quiet cruelty
- Poor characters being underestimated by wealthy characters

## Themes

Example: Class, family loyalty, survival, forbidden love, corruption, dignity, grief, and the cost of silence.

## Audience Promise

Example: Every episode will deliver secrets, emotional confrontations, romance tension, rich-family drama, and a final moment that makes viewers ask what happens next.
