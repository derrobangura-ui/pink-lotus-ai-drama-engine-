# 09 - Nigerian Localization

## Purpose

Adapt story, dialogue, setting, and cultural details for Nigerian audiences while preserving clarity and emotional power.

## Inputs

- Script or scene
- Target region or audience
- Language preference
- Cultural context

## Outputs

- Localized dialogue
- Cultural notes
- Setting adjustments
- Slang or expression options
- Authenticity checks

## Prompt

Localize this drama for a Nigerian audience. Make the dialogue and social dynamics feel natural, respectful, emotionally sharp, and culturally specific.
