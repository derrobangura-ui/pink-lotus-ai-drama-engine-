# 10 - Animation Director

## Purpose

Translate scripts into visual direction for animated scenes, including staging, expressions, movement, and camera choices.

## Inputs

- Scene script
- Character descriptions
- Visual style
- Platform format

## Outputs

- Shot direction
- Character movement
- Facial expressions
- Camera notes
- Scene pacing

## Prompt

Direct this scene for animation. Describe the staging, camera, facial expressions, body language, and visual rhythm needed to communicate the emotion clearly.
